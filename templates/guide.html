Guide

What you can do on this site, and what to expect.

Calendar

The calendar is the home page. It shows three months at a time, with each stay shown as a colored pill on the days it covers.

  • Each family has its own color, shown in the legend above the calendar.
  • Approved stays are shown in full color. Pending (not-yet-approved) stays are shown in gray.
  • A request that includes people from more than one family shows up as a separate colored pill for each family involved.
Requesting a stay

Use Requests → Submit Request to ask for a stay at Oakholm.

  • Pick a start and end date.
  • Under "Who's coming?", people are grouped by family, and within each family by household. Check a household's box to select everyone in it at once, or check people one at a time - both work together.
  • Enter your best estimate of the total number of occupants.

Once submitted, your request is pending until an approver reviews it. You'll see it on the calendar right away in gray.

Under Requests → Your Requests, you can see the status of everything you've submitted and edit any request (dates, attendees, occupant count) at any time, even after it's been approved.

Approving requests

If you're an approver, Requests → Approve Requests shows every pending request waiting on a decision.

  • Each request shows the requester, dates, attendees, and estimated occupants.
  • If a request's dates overlap another pending or approved request, you'll see a warning listing the conflicting request(s).
  • You can edit a request's details before approving or denying it, in case something needs adjusting first.
  • Approve or deny with one click - the requester's calendar updates immediately.
Email notifications

When someone submits a new request, approvers can get an email about it. What you receive is controlled by the notification setting on your Profile page:

  • Only requests from my own family (default) - you'll only hear about requests involving the family you head.
  • All requests, from any family - you'll hear about every new request, regardless of family.
  • Don't email me about requests - no request emails at all.
Checklists

Checklists are for things you want to walk through each time you're at Oakholm - opening up, closing down, restocking, and so on.

  • Checklists → + Create Checklist sets up a new reusable checklist with its own items.
  • Pick any checklist from the menu to run through it - check items off as you go, add photos, and jot down notes. Your progress is saved automatically as you go (notes save a few seconds after you stop typing, and the box flashes green when it's saved).
  • Each item has an edit (pencil) and delete (trash) icon. Deleting asks for confirmation first - the item disappears from future runs, but any completed run that already checked it off keeps showing it in its history.
  • When you're done, mark the run complete. It moves into Completed Checklists, where anyone can look back at past runs, their photos, and notes.
Notes

The Notes page is a shared board for anything worth leaving for the next people staying at Oakholm - a maintenance issue, a heads-up, a reminder.

  • Anyone can post a note.
  • You can edit or delete your own notes at any time.
  • Older notes can be archived to keep the board tidy (archiving is limited to admins).

When posting, you can mark a note as Lost & Found instead of General - use the All / General / Lost & Found tabs at the top of the page to filter the board.

Anyone can mark a Lost & Found item as claimed once it's been picked up (not just the person who posted it), which shows a "Claimed" badge on the note.

Feedback

Have a suggestion or run into a problem with the site? Use the Feedback link to send it in.

Your account

From the Account menu:

  • Profile - update your name, username, email, profile photo, and email notification preference.
  • Change Password - update your password. If you ever forget it, use "Forgot password?" on the login page to reset it by email.
